FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a DeskTime Trial?

When you sign up for a DeskTime trial for the first time, you'll receive two weeks of free access to all available features on our Premium plan, without limitations. At the end of the two weeks, you can choose to stay on Premium or downgrade to a lower tier plan.

How will I explain DeskTime to my teams?

The aim of DeskTime is not to monitor what your teams are doing. It's to monitor and measure how effective they are, so you can protect your business. Monitor automatically in the background or teach your teams to stay accountable with the interactive desktop app.

What are my billing options?

DeskTime offers monthly and annual billing options. Customers who pay annually receive two months free, a 16.6% discount on the monthly price. We accept payments from major credit card providers including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

Does DeskTime have a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a comprehensive refund policy. Please contact our support team for specific details about your situation and eligibility.

How long does it take to implement DeskTime?

Implementation is quick — most teams can get started within a day. For larger setups, we offer guided onboarding to ensure everything runs smoothly.

How can I cancel my DeskTime account?

If you need to cancel your DeskTime account, log in as the company owner, navigate to Settings / Billing, and look for the Cancel account link. After clicking, you'll be asked why you want to cancel before completing the process.
