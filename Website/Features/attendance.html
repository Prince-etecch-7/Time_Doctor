Frequently asked questions

How does automatic attendance tracking work? +
Time Doctor automatically logs when employees start and stop working. When they begin a task, they're clocked in. When they finish, they're clocked out. No manual entries required.
Can employees manually adjust their attendance? +
Yes, employees can submit edit requests for their time entries, which managers can approve or deny. All changes are logged in an audit trail for complete transparency.
Does it work for different time zones? +
Absolutely. Time Doctor handles multiple time zones automatically, showing each employee's local time and converting to your company's preferred time zone for reporting.
How do I export attendance data for payroll? +
You can export attendance reports in CSV, Excel, or PDF formats. We also offer direct integrations with major payroll systems like ADP, Gusto, and QuickBooks.
Is GPS tracking optional? +
Yes, GPS tracking is completely optional and only used if you enable it. It's useful for field teams but not required for standard office or remote workers.
